Game Rules

Objective 🎯

The main goal of Badam Satti is to be the first player to get rid of all your cards.

Setup ⚙️

  • The game is played with a standard 52-card deck. 🃏
  • All cards are dealt out as evenly as possible among the players. 👥🔄
  • The player with the 7 of Hearts (♥️7) starts the game. 🚀

Gameplay 🎮

  • The first player must play the 7 of Hearts.
  • On your turn, you can play a card if it builds upon the cards already on the table or you may choose to pass the turn . 🃏➡️
  • Cards are built up and down in sequence for each suit (e.g., if the ♥️7 is played, the next cards can be ♥️6 or ♥️8). ⬆️⬇️🔢
  • The 7s of other suits (♦️7, ♣️7, ♠️7) can be played at any time to start a new suit row. 💎✨
  • If you cannot play a valid card, you must pass your turn. 🚫⏭️

Winning the Game 🏆

The first player to play all of their cards is declared the winner. 🥇🎉 Scoring is based on the cards remaining in players' hands (lower is better). 📊👐
